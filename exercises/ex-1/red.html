Red

Red is a very strong color. It can mean anger, passionate, danger or even love. Some interesting facts about this color are:

~ "It is one of the top two favoite colors of all people" (colormatters.com)

~ "Red is the most popular color used on flags in the world. Approximately 77% of all flags include this color"

~ "The history of languages reveals that red is the first color after black and white"

When you mix this color with blue you then get the color violet - a new color that means something entirely different.
