Blue

"Blue is the favorite color of the people. It is cold, wet and slow as compared to red's warmth and intensity."

What's really interesting is that it has more meaning than any other color. Branching from this, different shades of blue can hold different meanings:

~ Dark blue --> "trust and intelligence"

~ Bright blue --> "cleanliness, strength, responsibility"

~ Light blue --> "peace, spiritual, infinity"

Again, you can mix blue with red to make a completely new color and this color is violet. One of my favorite colors.

Or, you can mix blue with yellow to make green. We could go even deeper and mix blue with green to make blue-green, but we should stay on track!
