Taste the Rainbow

You are about to embark on a colorful adventure. Color is all around us and it be can be both physical and non-physical. From elementary school we are taught the color wheel and how to make specific colors by mixing other colors. Since then, I have been addicted to colors and the meanings they hold and I want to take you through that journey. Choose and click on a color to learn more about it and begin the adventure!

As we all know, red is a common color. It is the color of apples and firetrucks. However, it can also related to emotions, such as anger.

Orange is the color of pumpkins, but emotionally it can mean excitement or warmth.

Yellow is the color of a banana, but emotionally it can mean happiness.

Now. For the adventure. As you continue throughout your informational journey of colors, keep an eye out for secret links to learn more about me and why colors are so important to my life. If you follow the secret links, you'll eventually find an ending secret!
